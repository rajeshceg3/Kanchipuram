Kanchipuram

Known as the "Golden City of a Thousand Temples," Kanchipuram is a living museum of Pallava, Chola, and Vijayanagara artistry. Explore the spiritual grandeur of this ancient capital, where sandstone marvels and towering gopurams map a sacred geography of Shiva, Vishnu, and Shakti. Discover the stories etched in stone that have withstood the test of time.
